About Us

Welcome to Gennii by Blendora Services Pvt. Ltd.!

Blendora Services Pvt. Ltd. is excited to announce the launch of Gennii—your ultimate shopping assistant! With Gennii, your shopping wishes are granted with just a tap of your finger. Whether you need daily essentials, luxury items, or unique finds, Gennii connects you to local stores, ensuring a delightful shopping experience.

What is Gennii?

Gennii is more than just a shopping app; it's a magical gateway to all your needs. Imagine a world where you can make a wish, and Gennii makes it happen. Our platform brings you closer to local stores, allowing you to browse, buy, and receive products swiftly. From groceries and electronics to fashion and home décor, Gennii has something for everyone.

Key Features

call/chat :

Directly communicate with store representatives via call or chat for any inquiries or assistance. Whether you need more details about a product or help with your order, our call/chat feature ensures you're never left wondering.

Buy :

Our seamless buying process enables you to purchase products with ease. Add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, and watch as your wishes come true. With Gennii, shopping is just a click away.

Search for Products :

Our powerful search engine helps you find exactly what you need. Simply type in the product you're looking for, and Gennii will present you with a comprehensive list of options from nearby stores.

Search for Stores:

Not sure where to shop? Explore and discover stores around you. Gennii provides detailed listings of local stores, making it easier for you to find the best places to shop.

Offers:

Stay updated with the latest offers, discounts, and promotions from your favorite stores. Gennii ensures you get the best deals, helping you save more while shopping for what you love.

Discover Nearby and City Stores:

With Gennii, you're not limited to local stores. Our unique "City" feature allows you to browse and buy products from stores in other cities as well. Now, you have access to an even broader range of products, no matter where you are.
