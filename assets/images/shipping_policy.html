Shipping Policy for Blendora Services Private Limited (Brand Name: Gennii)

Domestic Shipping:

  1. We offer free standard shipping on all national orders above ₹500.
  2. Orders are shipped within 2-3 business days.
  3. Delivery typically takes 5-7 business days.

International Shipping:

Currently, we do not ship internationally.

Order Tracking:

  1. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.
  2. You can track your order on our website using this tracking number.

Shipping Costs:

  1. Orders below ₹500 will incur a standard shipping fee of ₹50.
  2. Expedited shipping options are available at additional cost.

Delivery Issues:

If you encounter any delivery issues, please contact our support team at contact@gennii.com.

Address Changes:

Ensure correct address details at the time of order. Address changes post-shipping are not guaranteed.

Lost or Damaged Packages:

Contact us immediately if your package is lost or arrives damaged. We will work with the courier service to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
